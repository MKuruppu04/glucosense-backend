Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2024

Your privacy is critically important to us. GlucoSense is committed to protecting your personal health information and complying with all applicable privacy laws, including HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Physical address
  • Emergency contact information (guardians)

1.2 Health Information

We collect and store your health-related data, including:

  • Blood glucose measurements
  • Sensor readings (IR LED, RED LED, heart rate, SpO2)
  • Diabetes type and diagnosis date
  • Current medications and allergies
  • Healthcare provider information
  • Alert history and emergency notifications

1.3 Device Information

  • Raspberry Pi device ID and status
  • Device sync times and connection logs
  • App usage data and preferences

1.4 Technical Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device operating system
  • Access times and referring websites
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Health Monitoring: To track and analyze your glucose levels
  • Emergency Alerts: To send critical health notifications to you and your guardians
  • Account Management: To provide and maintain your account services
  • Communication: To send important updates about your health or account

2.2 Secondary Uses

  • Service Improvement: To enhance our platform and develop new features
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns (only if you consent)
  • Research: For medical research (anonymized data, only with explicit consent)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will NEVER sell your personal health information.

3.1 With Your Consent

  • Emergency Contacts: We share critical glucose alerts with your designated guardians
  • Healthcare Providers: If you authorize us to share data with your doctor

3.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third parties who assist us:

  • Twilio: For SMS and voice call emergency alerts
  • SendGrid: For email communications
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure database hosting
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud, or similar (encrypted)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protect our rights or property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect user safety in emergencies

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions limit who can view your data
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing (bcrypt) and JWT tokens
  • Audit Logging: All access to health data is logged for compliance
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments

4.2 Data Storage

  • Data stored in secure, HIPAA-compliant data centers
  • Regular encrypted backups
  • Geographic redundancy for disaster recovery

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Portability

  • View all your personal and health data anytime
  • Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON, CSV)
  • Request a complete copy of your information

5.2 Correction

  • Update your profile information anytime
  • Correct inaccurate glucose readings
  • Request correction of any errors in your data

5.3 Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

  • Request deletion of your account and all data
  • We will delete within 30 days of request
  • Some data may be retained for legal/compliance purposes
  • Anonymized aggregate data may be retained for research

5.4 Opt-Out

  • Disable non-critical alerts and notifications
  • Opt out of analytics and data sharing
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Children's Privacy

GlucoSense is intended for users 13 years and older. If you are under 18, you need parental consent to use our services. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Users

If you are accessing GlucoSense from outside your country, your data may be transferred to and processed in different locations. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Data Processing Agreements
  • Compliance with local privacy laws

8. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • After Deletion: Most data deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup data deleted within 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained for compliance (7 years)

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and security (always on)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage (only with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notified to you via email for material changes
  • Requires re-acceptance for significant changes

11. HIPAA Compliance

GlucoSense complies with HIPAA requirements:

  • We are a covered entity under HIPAA
  • Business Associate Agreements with all service providers
  • Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards in place
  • Breach notification procedures established
  • Privacy Officer designated
